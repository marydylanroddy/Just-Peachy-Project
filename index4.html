    • Aftercare



    Proper aftercare is essential to maintain the appearance and health of your gel and acrylic nails. Here are some tips for both:


    Gel Nail Aftercare


    1. Keep Them Dry: Avoid prolonged exposure to water for the first 24 hours to ensure the gel sets properly.
    2. Moisturise: Regularly apply cuticle oil and hand lotion to keep your nails and surrounding skin hydrated.
    3. Avoid Harsh Chemicals: Wear gloves when doing household chores or using cleaning products to protect your nails.
    4. Be Gentle: Avoid using your nails as tools to open things or scrape surfaces.
    5. Sun Protection: Apply sunscreen to your hands to protect your nails from UV damage.

    Acrylic Nail Aftercare


    1. Regular Fills: Schedule infills every 2-3 weeks to maintain the appearance and strength of your nails.
    2. Moisturise: Use cuticle oil daily to keep your nails nourished and prevent lifting.
    3. Avoid Heat: Stay away from heat sources like saunas or hot tubs for the first 24-48 hours after application.
    4. Protect Your Nails: Wear gloves when doing housework or gardening to prevent damage.
    5. Be Gentle: Like with gel nails, avoid using your acrylic nails as tools.
